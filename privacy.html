Spirals of Kemush Privacy Policy

Effective: April 30, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Spirals of Kemush mobile application ("the App") published by Basalt Sea Press.

What we collect

Nothing. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, location data, device identifiers, or any other data about you or your device.

What's stored on your device

The App saves the following data only on your device, never transmitted anywhere:

  • Which spiral panel you last viewed (so you can resume reading)
  • Which spirals you have completed
  • Your text size preference

This data can be cleared at any time from the App's Settings screen. Uninstalling the App also removes all of this data.

Network requests

The App does not make any network requests. It runs entirely offline. All content (the twelve spirals, panel artwork, and book text) ships with the App installer.

Third-party services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or telemetry services.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If the privacy policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at the URL above and the change will be summarized in the App's About screen.
