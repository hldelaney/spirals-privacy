Spirals of Kemush — Support

Thank you for reading. Spirals of Kemush is a quiet, offline reader app that contains the full text of The Book of Spirals by H. L. Delaney (Basalt Sea Press, 2025).

Contact

For questions, bug reports, or feedback, please email henry.delaney@gmail.com. Most messages are answered within a few days.

Common questions

How do I navigate? Tap the right side of any panel to advance, the left side to go back. Swipe left/right works the same way. The progress dots at the bottom show where you are within a spiral.

How do I resume reading? The app remembers the last panel you viewed in each spiral. When you reopen the app and tap a spiral, it returns to that panel automatically.

How do I change the text size? Tap Settings from the title screen and choose Small, Medium, or Large.

How does the read-aloud feature work? Tap the play icon in the reader to use your device's system voice. The voice may mispronounce some Klamath proper nouns; recorded narration in the author's own voice will arrive in a future update.

Does the app work offline? Yes. The app does not make any network requests. All twelve spirals, all panel artwork, and all book text ship with the app installer.

Does the app collect any data? No. See the Privacy Policy for details.
